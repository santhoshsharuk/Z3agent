At Z3Connect ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website (the "Site") and when you apply to become a commission-based agent through our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out the application form or interact with our Site, we may collect the following personal information ("Personal Data"):

  • Full name
  • Contact number (Mobile Number)
  • Email address
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Location/City
  • Educational details (highest qualification, field of study, institution, year of completion)
  • Current occupational background (e.g., student, freelancer)
  • Information about prior sales or promotion experience

  • Reasons for your interest in this opportunity

  • Any additional details voluntarily provided in your application or communications with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the Personal Data you provide to:

  • Process and review your application to become a Z3Connect Commission Agent.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application status, onboarding, and any related queries.
  • Provide you with training materials, guidance, and resources if your application is successful.
  • Administer the Z3Connect Agent Program.
  • Communicate updates about our services, policies, or terms.
  • Improve our recruitment process, Site functionality, and overall user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We **do not sell, rent, or trade** your Personal Data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your Personal Data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With authorized internal team members of Z3Connect who are involved in the application review and agent management process, on a need-to-know basis.
  • With service providers who assist us in our operations (e.g., hosting services), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Z3Connect, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your Personal Data on secure servers and take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction. However, no internet-based site can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We will retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (e.g., for legal, tax, or regulatory reasons).
