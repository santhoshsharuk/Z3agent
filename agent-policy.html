Z3Connect Commission Agent Policy

Effective Date: [Enter Date, e.g., October 26, 2023]

This document outlines the terms, responsibilities, and expectations for commission-based agents ("Agents") partnering with Z3Connect ("Company", "we", "us", "our"). By applying for, being accepted into, and working within the Z3Connect Commission Agent Program, you ("Agent", "you") agree to adhere to this policy.

1. Role Description and Responsibilities

Z3Connect commission agents are independent contractors responsible for:

  • Actively promoting and marketing Z3Connect's digital services, which may include but are not limited to: websites, web applications, e-commerce platforms, custom software, billing systems, smart billing solutions, management systems, AI chatbots, and online business solutions.
  • Generating qualified leads and identifying potential customers for Z3Connect's services.
  • Effectively communicating the value and benefits of Z3Connect's services to potential clients.
  • Facilitating the sales process by acting as a bridge between Z3Connect and potential clients, including initial consultations and information gathering as guided by Z3Connect.
  • Maintaining a professional and positive representation of the Z3Connect brand.
  • Adhering to all guidelines and training materials provided by Z3Connect.

2. Commission Structure and Payment

  • Agents will be compensated solely on a **commission basis** for each successful sale or client conversion directly attributable to their efforts, as verified by Z3Connect.
  • Specific commission rates, a percentage of the project value, or a fixed amount per service, will be clearly communicated to the Agent during the onboarding process and may vary based on the type of service sold and project complexity.
  • A "successful sale" or "client conversion" is defined as a client who signs a service agreement with Z3Connect and makes the initial required payment.
  • Commissions are typically paid out on a [e.g., monthly, bi-weekly - **Specify Your Payout Cycle**] basis.
  • Payments will be made via a mutually agreed method, such as bank transfer or UPI, after deduction of any applicable taxes or fees as required by law.
  • Z3Connect reserves the right to adjust commission structures with prior notice to Agents.

3. Training and Resources

  • Z3Connect will provide Agents with necessary training materials, including but not limited to: service information, sales pitch decks, FAQs, and guidance documents (e.g., PDFs).
  • Agents are expected to thoroughly review and understand all provided materials before commencing promotional activities.
  • Ongoing support and updates on services will be provided by Z3Connect as deemed necessary.

4. Work Mode and Relationship

  • This is a **freelance, part-time, remote opportunity**. Agents are independent contractors and not employees of Z3Connect.
  • This agreement does not create an employer-employee, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship between Z3Connect and the Agent.
  • Agents are not bound by fixed working hours and have the flexibility to manage their own schedule and work methods, provided they align with the professional standards set by Z3Connect.
  • Agents are responsible for their own tools, equipment (e.g., phone, laptop, internet), and any expenses incurred in performing their duties unless otherwise agreed in writing by Z3Connect.

5. Code of Conduct

Agents are expected to:

  • Represent Z3Connect in a professional, ethical, and honest manner at all times.
  • Provide accurate information about Z3Connect's services and capabilities. Avoid making false claims, guarantees, or misrepresentations.
  • Respect client confidentiality and handle all client information with discretion.
  • Not engage in any activity that could harm the reputation or business interests of Z3Connect.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations in their promotional activities.
  • Not use any Z3Connect intellectual property (logos, trademarks, copyrighted material) without prior written consent, except as provided in official marketing materials.

6. Data Protection and Confidentiality

  • Any client data, internal Z3Connect information, training materials, or proprietary business information shared with the Agent must be treated as strictly confidential.
  • Agents must not disclose, share, or misuse such confidential information for any purpose other than performing their duties under this agreement, either during or after their association with Z3Connect.
  • Agents must comply with all applicable data protection laws regarding any personal data they collect or process on behalf of Z3Connect.

7. Performance and Review

  • While there are no fixed sales quotas, Agents are encouraged to be proactive. Performance may be periodically reviewed.
  • Z3Connect may offer incentives or bonuses for exceptional performance at its discretion.
  • A certificate of experience may be provided after a certain period of active engagement and satisfactory performance (e.g., after 3-6 months), at Z3Connect's discretion.

8. Termination of Agreement

  • Either the Agent or Z3Connect may terminate this agreement with written notice (e.g., [Specify Notice Period, e.g., 7-14 days] notice).
  • Z3Connect reserves the right to immediately terminate the agreement and an Agent's status for:
    • Breach of any terms of this Agent Policy or the Code of Conduct.
    • Engaging in fraudulent activities, misrepresentation, or unethical practices.
    • Misuse of confidential information or Z3Connect's intellectual property.
    • Prolonged inactivity or consistent underperformance, as determined by Z3Connect after due communication.
  • Upon termination, the Agent must cease all promotional activities for Z3Connect and return any company property or confidential information.
  • Commissions for sales successfully closed and paid for prior to the termination date will be paid according to the agreed schedule, provided they were not a result of activities leading to termination for cause.

9. No Fixed Salary or Employment Benefits

  • It is explicitly understood that this is a **commission-based role only**. Agents will not receive any base salary, stipend, or employment benefits (such as health insurance, paid leave, retirement contributions, etc.) from Z3Connect.
  • Agents are solely responsible for their own tax obligations, including income tax, GST (if applicable), and any other statutory filings related to their earnings from commissions.

10. Agreement and Acknowledgment

By commencing work as a Z3Connect Commission Agent, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms and conditions outlined in this Z3Connect Commission Agent Policy.
  • You understand that you are an independent contractor and not an employee of Z3Connect.
  • You agree to conduct yourself in a manner consistent with the professional image and values of Z3Connect.

Z3Connect reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. Agents will be notified of any significant changes.
